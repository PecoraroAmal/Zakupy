Your Privacy Matters

Zakupy™ does not use cookies, trackers, or analytics. All your data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage.

We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. Your shopping list data remains completely private and under your control.

  • Data Storage: Local browser storage only
  • Cookies: None
  • Third-party services: None
  • Analytics: None
